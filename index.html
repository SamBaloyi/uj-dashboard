Video Defense Guidelines

Assessment 1 (7 min): Research topic & problem → Methodology understanding → Critical literature review approach → Reflection. Pass = 4/5 criteria met.

Assessment 2 (7 min): Sampling framework → Data collection method → Ethics & access → Critical evaluation of article. Pass = 4/6 criteria. Students who failed A1 video cannot resubmit.

Assessment 3 (5 min): Risk-based — only required if you score >75%, are flagged for possible AI use, or are randomly selected (~20%). Notified within 48 hours of submission. 5 working days to submit.

Assessment 4 (5–7 min): COMPULSORY for all. Present your complete research proposal: research question, methodology, literature, analysis plan, and reflection on your growth as a researcher.

Your Success Strategies

Proven approaches for excellence in your Advanced Diploma

The 3-Pass Learning System

Pass 1 – Pre-Class (30 min): Skim read to get the landscape. Note confusing parts to clarify in class.

Pass 2 – In Class: Active engagement. Ask questions. Take strategic notes on key insights.

Pass 3 – Post-Class (60 min): Deep review. Summarise in your own words. Create frameworks. Test understanding.

Why it works: Spaced repetition with increasing depth is scientifically proven to enhance long-term retention.

The Power Hour Technique

Every day, identify your most important academic task and dedicate ONE distraction-free hour to it: Phone off | Email closed | Social media blocked | Deep focus on ONE thing.

The Compound Effect: 365 hours of deep, focused work per year = your competitive advantage.

Remember: You don't need to be perfect. You need to be consistent. 1% improvement every day = 37× better in a year. Focus on systems, not goals. Trust the process.
